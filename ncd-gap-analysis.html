Condition Colonialism Index (CCI) = Africa's share of global disease burden (%) divided by Africa's share of trials vs the US (%). A CCI of 1.0 means proportional research investment. Values above 1.0 indicate under-researched conditions relative to burden. Values above 5.0 represent severe research colonialism.
