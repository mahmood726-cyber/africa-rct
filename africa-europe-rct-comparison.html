Strategic Insight

The analysis reveals that Europe focuses heavily on Cancer (30,204 trials) which represents specialized oncology research. Africa, while having a significant disease burden, shows high HIV and Maternal/Neonatal focus, but trails significantly in specialized areas like Cardiovascular (14.3x gap) and Mental Health.

Furthermore, the high termination rate in Africa (3.4%) compared to Europe (7.6%) suggests structural challenges in trial sustainability.
