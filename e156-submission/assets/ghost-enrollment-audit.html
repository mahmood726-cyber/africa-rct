4. The Invisible Patients

0.59%
Uganda's share of ghost-trial enrollment

Across 16 ghost trials enrolling 32,735 participants worldwide, Uganda contributes an estimated 192 patients.

These patients bear the risks of experimental treatment but their country has virtually no influence on trial design, endpoint selection, or post-trial drug pricing. The median ghost trial has 172 sites, making Uganda one data point among hundreds.

Combined with 39 mega-trials (est. 2,164 Ugandan participants), a total of 2,356 Ugandans are enrolled in trials where they constitute <5% of the study population.
