The Commandment of Equity

The 12 tribes of research data reveal a continent that is often used but rarely led. To 'part the sea' of inequity, we must move from Benjamin (The Lost/Unknown) to Judah (Local Leadership) and from Levi (The Infectious Orthodoxy) to a holistic health paradigm for all African people.
