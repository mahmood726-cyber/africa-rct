Methodology

Data source: ClinicalTrials.gov API v2 (queried 2026-03-28)

Filter: AREA[StudyType]INTERVENTIONAL AND AREA[OverallStatus]RECRUITING

Countries: 10 African nations (Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, Ethiopia, Egypt, Ghana, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Cameroon) + United States as comparator

Conditions: HIV, cancer, diabetes, hypertension, malaria -- the five conditions defining Africa's disease burden

Patient Desert Score: Count of conditions with zero recruiting trials per country (0-5 scale)

Capital concentration: Recruiting trials matching the capital city name vs country total

Limitation: Single registry; some trials may be registered elsewhere. Capital city proxy uses location name matching. Population data from UN 2024 estimates.

AI transparency: LLM assistance was used for drafting and language editing. The author reviewed and edited the manuscript and takes responsibility for the final content.
